About VII INK

VII INK is a premium tattoo studio based in London, Ontario, where Eastern craftsmanship meets Western creativity. The name "VII" — the Roman numeral for seven — represents completeness and the convergence of diverse artistic traditions. We specialize in culturally-rooted custom tattoo art, blending Chinese Traditional, Fine Line Minimalist, Dotwork, Black and Grey, and American Traditional styles into meaningful, one-of-a-kind pieces.

Your story. Two traditions. One studio.
